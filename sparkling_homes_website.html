End of Tenancy cleaning covers all booked rooms — bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living areas, hallways and any extras you select. You build your own package so you pay only for what's in your property. The base rate covers the core clean; each room and add-on is priced transparently on top.
Yes, absolutely. Every contractor in our network must hold a valid DBS certificate and active business insurance before being allocated any jobs. We verify these documents on onboarding and monitor expiry dates — no job is allocated to a contractor with expired compliance.
When you book, you select your service and then add rooms, windows, appliances and extras one by one. As you select each item, its price is immediately added to your running total — so you can see exactly what you're paying for at every stage. There are no hidden charges or surprise additions.
